Question

A survey investigated the relationship between the number of cleaners, \(n\), and the amount of time, \(t\), it takes them to clean a school.


Use your graphic display calculator to write down the equation of the regression line \(t\) on \(n\).

[2]
a.

Write down the value of the Pearson’s product–moment correlation coefficient, \(r\).

[2]
b.

Use your regression equation to find the amount of time 4 cleaners take to clean the school.

[2]
c.

Markscheme

\(t =  - 20.1n + 205\)

\(t = (-20.1046 \ldots )n + (204.755 \ldots )\)     (A1)(A1)     (C2)

 

Notes: Award (A1) for \(-20.1\) and \(205\) seen,

     (A1) for an equation involving \(t\) and \(n\).

 

[2 marks]

a.

\(-0.941 (-0.941366…)\)     (A2)     (C2)

 

Notes: Award (A0)(A1) for \( +0.941\).

 

[2 marks]

b.

\(-20.1046 \ldots  \times 4 + 204.755 \ldots \)     (M1)

 

Note: Award (M1) for substitution into their regression equation.

 

\(124\) (minutes) (\(124.337…\))     (A1)(ft)     (C2)

 

Notes: Follow through from their regression equation found in part (a). Accept \(125\) (minutes) (\(124.6\)).
