\({\text{Perpendicular gradient}} = - \frac{4}{5}\)     (A1)(ft)

Equation of perpendicular bisector is

\(y = - \frac{4}{5}x + c\)

\(3 = - \frac{4}{5}(2) + c\)     (M1)
