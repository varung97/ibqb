Question

Consider the function \(f\left( x \right) = \frac{{{x^4}}}{4}\).

Find f'(x)

[1]
a.

Find the gradient of the graph of f at \(x =  - \frac{1}{2}\).

[2]
b.

Find the x-coordinate of the point at which the normal to the graph of f has gradient \({ - \frac{1}{8}}\).

[3]
c.
