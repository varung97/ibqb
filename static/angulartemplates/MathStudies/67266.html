Question

Consider the functions \(f(x) = x + 1\) and \(g(x) = {3^x} - 2\).

Write down

(i)     the \(x\)-intercept of the graph of \(y = {\text{ }}f(x)\);

(ii)     the \(y\)-intercept of the graph of \(y = {\text{ }}g(x)\).

[2]
a.

Solve \(f(x) = g(x)\).

[2]
b.

Write down the interval for the values of \(x\) for which \(f(x) > g(x)\).

[2]
c.
