Part of the graph of \(y = f(x)\) is shown on the axes below. The graph of the function has its local maximum at \(( - 2,{\text{ }} - 2)\) and its local minimum at \((2,{\text{ }}6)\).

Write down the domain of the function.

[2]
c.
