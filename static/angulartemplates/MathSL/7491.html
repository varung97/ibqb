Question

In an arithmetic sequence, \({u_1} = 2\) and \({u_3} = 8\) .

Find d .

[2]
a.

Find \({u_{20}}\) .

[2]
b.

Find \({S_{20}}\) .

[2]
c.

Markscheme

attempt to find d     (M1)

e.g. \(\frac{{{u_3} - {u_1}}}{2}\) , \(8 = 2 + 2d\)

\(d = 3\)     A1     N2

[2 marks]

a.

correct substitution     (A1)

e.g. \({u_{20}} = 2 + (20 - 1)3\) , \({u_{20}} = 3 \times 20 - 1\)

\({u_{20}} = 59\)     A1     N2

[2 marks]

correct substitution     (A1)
