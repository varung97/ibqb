Question

Let \(f(x) = 6x\sqrt {1 - {x^2}} \), for \( - 1 \leqslant x \leqslant 1\), and \(g(x) = \cos (x)\), for \(0 \leqslant x \leqslant \pi \).

Let \(h(x) = (f \circ g)(x)\).

Write \(h(x)\) in the form \(a\sin (bx)\), where \(a,{\text{ }}b \in \mathbb{Z}\).

a.

Hence find the range of \(h\).
