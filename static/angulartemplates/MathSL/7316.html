Question

Let \(f(x) = 7 - 2x\) and \(g(x) = x + 3\) .

Find \((g \circ f)(x)\) .

[2]
a.

Write down \({g^{ - 1}}(x)\) .

[1]
b.

Find \((f \circ {g^{ - 1}})(5)\) .

[2]
c.

Markscheme

attempt to form composite     (M1)

e.g. \(g(7 - 2x)\) , \(7 - 2x + 3\)

\((g \circ f)(x) = 10 - 2x\)     A1     N2

[2 marks]

a.

\({g^{ - 1}}(x) = x - 3\)     A1     N1

[1 mark]

b.

METHOD 1

valid approach     (M1)

e.g. \({g^{ - 1}}(5)\) , \(2\) , \(f(5)\)

\(f(2) = 3\)     A1     N2

METHOD 2

attempt to form composite of f and \({g^{ - 1}}\)     (M1)

e.g. \((f \circ {g^{ - 1}})(x) = 7 - 2(x - 3)\) , \(13 - 2x\)

\((f \circ {g^{ - 1}})(5) = 3\)     A1     N2

[2 marks]
