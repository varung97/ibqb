Question

Let \(f(x) = 3{(x + 1)^2} - 12\) .

Show that \(f(x) = 3{x^2} + 6x - 9\) .

[2]
a.

For the graph of f

(i)     write down the coordinates of the vertex;

(ii)    write down the equation of the axis of symmetry;

(iii)   write down the y-intercept;

(iv)   find both x-intercepts.

[8]
b(i), (ii), (iii) and (iv).

Hence sketch the graph of f .
