Question

Consider \(f(x) = {x^2} + \frac{p}{x}\) , \(x \ne 0\) , where p is a constant.

Find \(f'(x)\) .

[2]
a.

There is a minimum value of \(f(x)\) when \(x = - 2\) . Find the value of \(p\) .

[4]
b.

Markscheme

\(f'(x) = 2x - \frac{p}{{{x^2}}}\)     A1A1     N2

Note: Award A1 for \(2x\) , A1 for \( - \frac{p}{{{x^2}}}\) .
