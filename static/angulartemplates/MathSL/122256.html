Question

Let \(x = \ln 3\) and \(y = \ln 5\). Write the following expressions in terms of \(x\) and \(y\).

\(\ln \left( {\frac{5}{3}} \right)\).

[2]
a.

\(\ln 45\).

[4]
b.

Markscheme

correct approach     (A1)

eg\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\(\ln 5 - \ln 3\)

\(\ln \left( {\frac{5}{3}} \right) = y - x\)    A1     N2

[2 marks]

a.
