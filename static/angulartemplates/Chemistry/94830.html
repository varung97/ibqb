Question

A.     \({\text{C}}{{\text{H}}_{\text{2}}}{\text{O}}\)

B.     \({\text{C}}{{\text{H}}_{\text{4}}}{\text{O}}\)

D.     \({{\text{C}}_{\text{2}}}{{\text{H}}_{\text{4}}}{{\text{O}}_{\text{2}}}\)

Markscheme
