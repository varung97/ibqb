Question

\[{\text{2A(g)}} + {\text{3B(g)}} \rightleftharpoons {\text{C(g)}} + {\text{3D(g)}}\]

A.     \(\frac{1}{6}\)

B.     \(\frac{9}{7}\)

C.     3

D.     6

Markscheme

D
