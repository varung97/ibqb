Question

What is the enthalpy of solution of MgF2(s) in kJ mol−1?

Lattice enthalpy of MgF2(s) = 2926 kJ mol−1

Hydration enthalpy of Mg2+(g) = −1963 kJ mol−1

Hydration enthalpy of F−(g) = −504 kJ mol−1

A.     2926 − 1963 + 2(−504)

B.     2926 − 1963 − 504

C.     −2926 − (−1963) − (−504)

D.     −2926 − (−1963) − 2(−504)

Markscheme

A
