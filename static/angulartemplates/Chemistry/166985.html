Question

What is the order of decreasing reactivity of the metals (most reactive first)?

Zn(s) + Sn2+(aq) → Zn2+(aq) + Sn(s)
Cu(s) + Zn2+(aq) → No Reaction
Sn(s) + Cu2+(aq) → Sn2+(aq) + Cu(s)
Ag(s) + Cu2+(aq) → No Reaction

A.     Zn > Cu > Sn > Ag

B.     Sn > Zn > Ag > Cu

C.     Ag > Cu > Zn > Sn

D.     Zn > Sn > Cu > Ag

Markscheme

D
