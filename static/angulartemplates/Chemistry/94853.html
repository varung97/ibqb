Question

Which statement is correct for the electrolysis of molten lead iodide, \({\text{Pb}}{{\text{I}}_{\text{2}}}\)?

A.     Chemical energy is converted into electrical energy.

B.     \({\text{P}}{{\text{b}}^{2 + }}\) ions are oxidized at the negative electrode (cathode).

C.     \({{\text{I}}_{\text{2}}}\) is produced at the positive electrode (anode).

D.     Ions are produced at both electrodes.

Markscheme

C
