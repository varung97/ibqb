Propagation:

Cl• + CH4 → Cl–H + •CH3

Do not penalize missing electron dot on radicals if consistent throughout.

Accept Cl2, HCl and CH3Cl without showing bonds.

Do not accept hydrogen radical, H• or H, but apply ECF to other propagation steps.
