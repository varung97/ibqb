Question

Methyl orange is an indicator which changes its colour from red to yellow in a pH range of 3.2 – 4.4.

For which titration would methyl orange be a suitable indicator?

A.     Iodine and sodium thiosulfate solution

B.     Hydrochloric acid and ammonia solution

C.     Ethanoic acid and sodium hydroxide solution

D.     Ethanoic acid and ammonia solution

Markscheme

B
