b.vi.

Ka = \({10^{ - 4.35}}/4.46683 \times {10^{ - 5}}\)

[H3O+] = \(\sqrt {4.46683 \times {{10}^{ - 5}} \times 1.60 \times {{10}^{ - 3}}} \,\,\,/\,\,\sqrt {7.1469 \times {{10}^{ - 8}}} \,\,/\,\,2.6734 \times {10^{ - 4}}\) «mol dm–3»

pH = «\( - \log \sqrt {7.1469 \times {{10}^{ - 8}}}  = \)» 3.57
