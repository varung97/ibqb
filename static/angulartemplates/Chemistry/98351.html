Question

Which of the following is consistent with Avogadro’s law?

A.     \(\frac{P}{T} = \) constant (\(V\), \(n\) constant)

B.     \(\frac{V}{T} = \) constant (\(P\), \(n\) constant)

C.     \(Vn = \) constant (\(P\), \(T\) constant)

D.     \(\frac{V}{n} = \) constant (\(P\), \(T\) constant)

Markscheme

D
