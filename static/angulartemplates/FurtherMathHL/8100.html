AF : FB .

using Ceva's theorem,

\(\frac{{{\rm{BD}}}}{{{\rm{DC}}}} \times \frac{{{\rm{CE}}}}{{{\rm{EA}}}} \times \frac{{{\rm{AF}}}}{{{\rm{FB}}}} = 1\)     M1A1

\(\frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{1} \times \frac{{{\rm{AF}}}}{{{\rm{FB}}}} = 1\)     A1

\(\frac{{{\rm{AF}}}}{{{\rm{FB}}}} = \frac{3}{8}\) or AF : FB \( = 3 : 8\)     A1
