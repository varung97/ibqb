it follows that \({x^{ - 1}}y{\text{ }}{y^{ - 1}}z = {x^{ - 1}}z \in H \Rightarrow xRz\)     M1A1

therefore transitive (therefore \(R\) is an equivalence relation on the set  \(G\))     AG
