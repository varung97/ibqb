\( = \frac{1}{{{n^2}}}\left( {np(1 - p) + {n^2}{p^2}} \right)\)     A1

\( \ne {p^2}\)     A1

therefore not unbiased     AG

(c)     \({\text{E}}\left[ {\left( {\frac{{X(X - 1)}}{{n(n - 1)}}} \right)} \right] = \frac{{{\text{E}}({X^2}) - {\text{E}}(X)}}{{n(n - 1)}}\)     M1

\( = \frac{{np(1 - p) + {n^2}{p^2} - np}}{{n(n - 1)}}\)     A1

\( = \frac{{n{p^2}(n - 1)}}{{n(n - 1)}}\)     A1

\( = {p^2}\)
