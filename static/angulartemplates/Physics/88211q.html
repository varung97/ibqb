Question

A body X of emissivity e is at temperature T1. X is inside a box whose walls act as a black body of temperature T2. T1 is greater than T2.

What is the net intensity of radiation leaving body X?

A. \(\sigma {T_1}^4\)

B. \(e\sigma {T_1}^4\)

C. \(e\sigma {T_1}^4 - \sigma {T_2}^4\)

D. \(e\sigma \left( {{T_1}^4 - {T_2}^4} \right)\)
