Question

Wind of speed v is incident normally on a wind turbine of radius r. The maximum theoretical power output of the turbine is P. For wind of speed 2v incident normally on a similar turbine of radius \(\frac{1}{2}\)r, the maximum theoretical power will be

A. \(\frac{1}{2}\)P.
B. P.
C. 2P.
D. 4P.

Markscheme

C
