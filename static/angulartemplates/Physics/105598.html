Question

A football is kicked with an initial velocity \(u\) at an angle \(\theta \) to the horizontal and reaches the ground \(t\) seconds later.

Ignoring air resistance what is the range \(R\) of the football?

A.     \(ut\)

B.     \(ut\cos \theta \)

C.     \(ut\sin \theta \)

D.     \(ut\tan \theta \)

Markscheme

B
