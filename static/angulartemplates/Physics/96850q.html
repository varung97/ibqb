Question

The ratio \(\frac{{{N_{\rm{S}}}}}{{{N_{\rm{T}}}}}\) is

A. 1

B. \(\frac{{{\lambda _{\rm{S}}}}}{{{\lambda _{\rm{T}}}}}\)

C. \(\frac{{{\lambda _{\rm{T}}}}}{{{\lambda _{\rm{S}}}}}\)

D. λSλT
