Question

A transverse travelling wave has an amplitude \({x_0}\) and wavelength \(\lambda \). What is the minimum distance between a crest and a trough measured in the direction of energy propagation?

A.     \(2{x_0}\)

B.     \({x_0}\)

C.     \(\lambda \)

D.     \(\frac{\lambda }{2}\)

Markscheme

D
