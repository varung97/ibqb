Markscheme

\(\frac{{{L_{\rm{K}}}}}{{{L_{\rm{S}}}}} = {\left[ {\frac{{{m_{\rm{K}}}}}{{{m_{\rm{S}}}}}} \right]^{3.5}}\);
\(m = {\left[ {\frac{{{L_{\rm{K}}}}}{{{L_{\rm{S}}}}} \times {m_{\rm{S}}}^{3.5}} \right]^{\frac{1}{{3.5}}}} = {\left[ {2.0 \times {{10}^4}} \right]^{\frac{1}{{3.5}}}}{m_{\rm{S}}}{\rm{ = 16.9}}{{\rm{m}}_{\rm{S}}}\);
hence mK≈17mS
