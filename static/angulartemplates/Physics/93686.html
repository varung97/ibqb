Question

What is the acceleration of an object rotating with constant speed v in a circle of radius r?

A. Zero

B. \(\frac{{{v^2}}}{r}\) towards the centre of the circle

C. \(\frac{{{v^2}}}{r}\) away from the centre of the circle

D. \(\frac{{{v^2}}}{r}\) along a tangent to the circle

Markscheme

B
