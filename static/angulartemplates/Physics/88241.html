Question

A truck is pulled up an inclined plane at constant speed by an electric motor. The gain in potential energy of the truck is 48 kJ. The efficiency of the electric motor is \(\frac{2}{3}\).

How much energy is dissipated in pulling the truck up the plane?

A. 16 kJ

B. 24 kJ

C. 32 kJ

D. 64 kJ

Markscheme

B
