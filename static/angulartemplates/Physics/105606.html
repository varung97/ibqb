Question

The diagram below shows a particle with positive charge q accelerating between two conducting plates at potentials \({V_1}\) and \({V_2}\).

Which of the following is the kinetic energy gained by the charge in moving between the plates?

A.     \({V_2}q\)

B.     \({V_1}q\)

C.     \(({V_1} - {V_2})q\)

D.     \(({V_2} - {V_1})q\)

Markscheme

C
