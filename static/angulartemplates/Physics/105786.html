Question

Two resistors, made of the same material, are connected in series to a battery. The length of resistor X is twice that of resistor Y, and X has twice the cross-sectional area of Y.

Which of the following gives \(\frac{{{\text{resistance of X}}}}{{{\text{resistance of Y}}}}\)?

A.     \(\frac{1}{4}\)

B.     \(\frac{1}{2}\)

C.     1

D.     4

Markscheme

C
