Question

An alternating current supply of negligible internal resistance is connected to two resistors that are in parallel.

The resistance of each resistor is \(R\) and the peak voltage of the ac supply is \({V_0}\). Which of the following is the average power dissipated in the circuit?

A.     \(\frac{{2V_0^2}}{R}\)

B.     \(\frac{{V_0^2}}{R}\)

C.     \(\frac{{V_0^2}}{{\sqrt 2 R}}\)

D.     \(\frac{{V_0^2}}{{2R}}\)

 

Markscheme

B
