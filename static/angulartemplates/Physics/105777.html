Question

A gas atom strikes a wall with speed \(v\) at an angle \(\theta \) to the normal to the wall. The atom rebounds at the same speed \(v\) and angle \(\theta \).

Which of the following gives the magnitude of the momentum change of the gas atom?

A.     zero

B.     \(2mv\sin \theta \)

C.     \(2mv\)

D.     \(2mv\cos \theta \)

Markscheme

D
