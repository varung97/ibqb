Question

Electrons, each with a charge e, move with speed v along a metal wire. The electric current in the wire is I.

Plane P is perpendicular to the wire. How many electrons pass through plane P in each second?

A.  \(\frac{e}{I}\)

B.  \(\frac{{ve}}{I}\)

C.  \(\frac{I}{{ve}}\)

D.  \(\frac{I}{e}\)
