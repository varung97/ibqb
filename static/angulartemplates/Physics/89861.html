Question

The average surface temperature of Mars is about 200 K. The average surface temperature of Earth is about 300 K. Both can be regarded as black bodies.

What is the ratio \(\frac{{{\text{energy radiated per second per unit area on Mars}}}}{{{\text{energy radiated per second per unit area on Earth}}}}\)?

A.     0.7

B.     0.4

C.     0.3

D.     0.2

Markscheme

D
