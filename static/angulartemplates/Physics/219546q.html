The value of cos 30˚ is \(\frac{{\sqrt 3 }}{2}\). What is the intensity of the light emerging through the second filter?

A. \(\frac{{\sqrt 3 }}{2}\)I0

B. \(\frac{3}{2}\)I0

C. \(\frac{3}{4}\)I0

D. \(\frac{3}{8}\)I0
