A bar magnet is close to a coil. No other magnetic fields are present. An ammeter is connected to the coil.

The magnet and the coil are moved in the following ways.

I. The magnet and the coil both move to the right with the same speed.
II. The magnet is stationary and the coil moves to the left.
III. The coil is stationary and the magnet moves to the right.

In which of the following will the ammeter indicate a current?

A. I and II only
B. I and III only
C. II and III only
D. I only
