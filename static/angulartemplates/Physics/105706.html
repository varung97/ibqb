Question

Two positive and two negative point charges of equal magnitude are placed at the vertices of a square as shown. The origin of the axes is at the centre of the square.

The electric potential is zero

A.     at the origin of the axes only.

B.     along both the \(x\)-axis and the \(y\)-axis.

C.     along the \(y\)-axis only.

D. along the \(x\)-axis only.

Markscheme

B
