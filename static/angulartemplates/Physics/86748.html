Question

The initial number of atoms in a pure radioactive sample is N. The radioactive half-life of the sample is defined as the

A. time taken for one atom to undergo decay.

B. probability for \(\frac{N}{2}\) atoms to undergo decay.

C. time taken for \(\frac{N}{2}\) atoms to undergo decay.

D. probability that one atom will decay per unit time.

Markscheme

C
