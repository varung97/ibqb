Question

What is the magnitude of the electric field strength at P?

A.     \(\frac{1}{{\pi {\varepsilon _0}{x^2}}}\)

B.     \(\frac{1}{{2\pi {\varepsilon _0}{x^2}}}\)

C.     \(\frac{1}{{4\pi {\varepsilon _0}{x^2}}}\)

D.     \(\frac{1}{{7\pi {\varepsilon _0}{x^2}}}\)
