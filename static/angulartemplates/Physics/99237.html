Question

This question is about relativistic mechanics.

A proton, after acceleration from rest through a potential difference V, has momentum 1600MeVc–1.

Calculate the value of V.

Markscheme

\(E = \sqrt {{p^2}{c^2} + {{\left( {m{c^2}} \right)}^2}}  = \sqrt {{{1600}^2} + {{938}^2}}  \approx 1855\left( {{\rm{MeV}}} \right)\);
ΔE=1855-938=917(MeV);
potential difference, V≈920(MV); (this marking point must be explicit)
