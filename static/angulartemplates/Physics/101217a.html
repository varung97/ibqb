\(v = \frac{d}{T}\)=H0d therefore \(T = \frac{1}{{{H_0}}}\);

(ii) \(T = \frac{1}{{80}} \times \frac{{{{10}^6} \times 3.26 \times 9.46 \times {{10}^{15}}}}{{1000}} = 4 \times {10^{17}}\left( {\rm{s}} \right)\);
