Question

An object at the end of a spring oscillates vertically with simple harmonic motion. The graph shows the variation with time \(t\) of the displacement \(x\). The amplitude is \({x_0}\) and the period of oscillation is \(T\).

Which of the following is the correct expression for the maximum acceleration of the object?

A.     \(\frac{{2\pi }}{T}{x_0}\)

B.     \(\frac{{2\pi }}{{{T^2}}}{x_0}\)

C.     \(\frac{{4{\pi ^2}}}{{{T^2}}}{x_0}\)

D.     \(\frac{{4{\pi ^2}}}{T}{x_0}\)

Markscheme

C
