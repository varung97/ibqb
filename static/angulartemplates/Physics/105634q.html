Question

A.     \(\frac{{60 \times 100}}{{0.1 \times 40}}\)

B.     \(\frac{{60 \times 0.1}}{{40}}\)

C.     \(\frac{{0.1 \times 40}}{{60}}\)

D.     \(\frac{{60}}{{40}}\)
