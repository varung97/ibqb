After acceleration, the de Broglie wavelength of X is λX and that of Y is λY. The speeds reached by the electrons are well below that of the speed of light.

What is the ratio \(\frac{{{\lambda _{\rm{X}}}}}{{{\lambda _{\rm{Y}}}}}\)?

A. 2

B. \(\sqrt 2 \)

C. \(\frac{1}{2}\)

D. \(\frac{1}{{\sqrt 2 }}\)
