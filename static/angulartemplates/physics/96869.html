Question

A car on a road follows a horizontal circular path at constant speed. Which of the following correctly identifies the origin and the direction of the net force on the car?

Markscheme

D

Examiners report

This was well done by the candidates who correctly identified the origin of the force as the frictional force of the road on the tyres.
