\(\frac{{{u_{n + 1}}}}{{{u_n}}} = \frac{{{{(n + 1)}^{10}}}}{{{{10}^{n + 1}}}} \times \frac{{{{10}^n}}}{{{n^{10}}}}\)     M1A1

\( = \frac{1}{{10}}{\left( {1 + \frac{1}{n}} \right)^{10}}\)     A1
