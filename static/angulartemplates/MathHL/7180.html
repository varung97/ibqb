Question

Consider the arithmetic sequence 8, 26, 44, \( \ldots \) .

(a)     Find an expression for the \({n^{{\text{th}}}}\) term.

(b)     Write down the sum of the first n terms using sigma notation.

(c)     Calculate the sum of the first 15 terms.

Markscheme

(a)     \(18n - 10\,\,\,\,\,\)(or equivalent)     A1

 

(b)     \(\sum\limits_1^n {(18r - 10)\,\,\,\,\,} \)(or equivalent)     A1

 

(c)     by use of GDC or algebraic summation or sum of an AP     (M1)

\(\sum\limits_1^{15} {(18r - 10) = 2010} \)     A1

[4 marks]
