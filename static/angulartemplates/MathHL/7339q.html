Question

Let R be a relation on the set \(\mathbb{Z}\) such that \(aRb \Leftrightarrow ab \geqslant 0\), for a, b \( \in \mathbb{Z}\).

(a)     Determine whether R is

(i)     reflexive;

(ii)     symmetric;

(iii)     transitive.

(b)     Write down with a reason whether or not R is an equivalence relation.
