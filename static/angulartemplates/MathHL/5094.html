(i)     Write down \({z_1}\) in Cartesian form.

(ii)     Hence determine \({({z_1} + {z_2})^ * }\) in Cartesian form.

a.

(i)     Write \({z_2}\) in modulus-argument form.

(ii)     Hence solve the equation \({z^3} = {z_2}\) .

(i)     if \({z^2} = {(1 + {z_2})^2}\);
