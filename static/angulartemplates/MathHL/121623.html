Question

Two events \(A\) and \(B\) are such that \({\text{P}}(A \cap B') = 0.2\) and \({\text{P}}(A \cup B) = 0.9\).

On the Venn diagram shade the region \(A' \cap B'\).

[1]
a.

Find \({\text{P}}(A'|B')\).

[4]
b.

Markscheme

     A1

[1 mark]

a.

\(P(A'|B') = \frac{{P(A' \cap B')}}{{P(B')}}\)    (M1)

\(P(B') = 0.1 + 0.2 = 0.3\)    (A1)

\(P(A' \cap B') = 0.1\)    (A1)

\(P(A'|B') = \frac{{0.1}}{{0.3}} = \frac{1}{3}\)    A1
