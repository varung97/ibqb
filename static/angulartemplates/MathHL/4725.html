Question

Expand and simplify \({\left( {x - \frac{2}{x}} \right)^4}\).

[3]
a.

Hence determine the constant term in the expansion \((2{x^2} + 1){\left( {x - \frac{2}{x}} \right)^4}\).

[2]
b.
