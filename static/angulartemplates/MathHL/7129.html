(i)     Find the cosine of the angle between vectors a and b.

(ii)     Find a \( \times \) b.

(iv)     The plane \(\prod \) intersects the x-y plane in the line l. Find the area of the finite triangular region enclosed by l, the x-axis and the y-axis.
