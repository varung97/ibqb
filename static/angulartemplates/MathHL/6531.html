Question

Given that (x − 2) is a factor of \(f(x) = {x^3} + a{x^2} + bx - 4\) and that division \(f(x)\) by (x − 1) leaves a remainder of −6 , find the value of a and the value of b .

Markscheme

\(f(2) = 8 + 4a + 2b - 4 = 0\)     M1

\( \Rightarrow 4a + 2b = - 4\)     A1

\(f(1) = 1 + a + b - 4 = - 6\)     M1

\( \Rightarrow a + b = - 3\)     A1

solving, \(a = 1,{\text{ }}b = - 4\)     A1A1
