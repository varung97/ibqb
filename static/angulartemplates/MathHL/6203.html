Question

Consider the complex numbers \(z = 1 + 2{\text{i}}\) and \(w = 2 + a{\text{i}}\) , where \(a \in \mathbb{R}\) .

Find a when

(a)     \(\left| w \right| = 2\left| z \right|;\) ;

(b)     \({\text{Re}}(zw) = 2\operatorname{Im} (zw)\) .

\(\left| w \right| = 2\left| z \right|\)
