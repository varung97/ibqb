Question

The binary operation \( * \) is defined for \(a{\text{, }}b \in {\mathbb{Z}^ + }\) by

\[a * b = a + b - 2.\]

(a)     Determine whether or not \( * \) is

(i)     closed,

(ii)     commutative,

(iii)     associative.

(b)     (i)     Find the identity element.

(ii)     Find the set of positive integers having an inverse under \( * \).
