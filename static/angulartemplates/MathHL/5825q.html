\[{r_1} = \left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}

  7
\end{array}} \right) + m\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}

\end{array}} \right)\]

\[{r_2} = \left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}
