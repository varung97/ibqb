METHOD 1

for \(x > 4\)

\((x + 2)(x - 4) = 7\)     (M1)

\({x^2} - 2x - 8 = 7 \Rightarrow {x^2} - 2x - 15 = 0\)

\((x - 5)(x + 3) = 0\)

\(({\text{as }}x > 4{\text{ then}}){\text{ }}x = 5\)     A1

Note:     Award A0 if \(x =  - 3\) is also given as a solution.

for \(x < 4\)

\((x + 2)(x - 4) =  - 7\)     M1

\( \Rightarrow {x^2} - 2x - 1 = 0\)

\(x = \frac{{2 \pm \sqrt {4 + 4} }}{2} = 1 \pm \sqrt 2 \)     (M1)A1
