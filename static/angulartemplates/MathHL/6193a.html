(i)     if \( * \) is commutative \(a * b = b * a\)

since \(a + b + 1 = b + a + 1\) , \( * \) is commutative     R1

 

(ii)     let e be the identity element

\(a * e = a + e + 1 = a\)     M1

\( \Rightarrow e = - 1\)     A1

 

(iii)     let a have an inverse, \({a^{ - 1}}\)

\(a * {a^{ - 1}} = a + {a^{ - 1}} + 1 = - 1\)     M1

\( \Rightarrow {a^{ - 1}} = - 2 - a\)     A1
