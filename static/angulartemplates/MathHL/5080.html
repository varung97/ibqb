\(\int_0^1 {f(x)dx = 1} \)     (M1)

\( \Rightarrow a = \frac{1}{{\int_0^1 {{e^{ - x}}dx} }}\)

\( \Rightarrow a = \frac{1}{{\left[ { - {e^{ - x}}} \right]_0^1}}\)

\( \Rightarrow a = \frac{e}{{e - 1}}\) (or equivalent)     A1
