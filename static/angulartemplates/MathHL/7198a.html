METHOD 2

with an AP the mean of an even number of consecutive terms equals the mean of the middle terms     (M1)

\(\frac{{{a_{10}} + {a_{11}}}}{2} = 16\,\,\,\,\,{\text{(or }}{a_{10}} + {a_{11}} = 32)\)     A1

\(\frac{{{a_5} + {a_6}}}{2} = 6\,\,\,\,\,{\text{(or }}{a_5} + {a_6} = 12)\)     A1

\({a_{10}} - {a_5} + {a_{11}} - {a_6} = 20\)     M1

\(5d + 5d = 20\)

\(d = 2{\text{ and }}a = - 3\,\,\,\,\,{\text{(or }}{a_5} = 5{\text{ or }}{a_{10}} = 15)\)     A1
