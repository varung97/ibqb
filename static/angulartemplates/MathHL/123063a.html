we need to show that \(f(a * b) = f(a) * f(b)\)     R1

Note:     This R1 may be awarded at any stage.

let \(a,{\text{ }}b \in G\)     (M1)

consider \(f(a) * f(b)\)     M1

\( = {a^{ - 1}} * {b^{ - 1}}\)    A1

consider \(f(a * b) = {(a * b)^{ - 1}}\)     M1

\( = {b^{ - 1}} * {a^{ - 1}}\)    A1

\( = {a^{ - 1}} * {b^{ - 1}}\) since \(G\) is Abelian     R1

hence \(f\) is an isomorphism     AG
