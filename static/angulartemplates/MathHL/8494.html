Question

The function \(f(x) = 4{x^3} + 2ax - 7a\) , \(a \in \mathbb{R}\), leaves a remainder of \(−10\) when divided by \(\left( {x - a} \right)\) .

Find the value of \(a\) .

[3]
a.

Show that for this value of \(a\) there is a unique real solution to the equation \(f (x) = 0\) .

[2]
b.

\(a = - 2\)     A1
