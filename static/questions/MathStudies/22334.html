Question

Consider the propositions

     \(p\): I have a bowl of soup.

     \(q\): I have an ice cream.

Write down, in words, the compound proposition \(\neg p \Rightarrow q\).

[2]
a.

Complete the truth table.


[2]
b.

Write down, in symbolic form, the converse of \(\neg p \Rightarrow q\).

[2]
c.

Markscheme

If I do not have a bowl of soup then I have an ice cream.     (A1)(A1)     (C2)

 

Notes: Award (A1) for If… then…

     Award (A1) for correct statements in correct order.

 

[2 marks]

a.

     (A1)(A1)(ft)     (C2)

 

Note: Follow through from third column to fourth column.

 

[2 marks]

b.

\(q \Rightarrow \neg p\)     (A1)(A1)     (C2)

 

Notes: Award (A1) for \( \Rightarrow \).

     Award (A1) for \(q\) and \(\neg p\) in correct order.

     Accept \(\neg p \Leftarrow q\).

 

[2 marks]

c.

Examiners report

Most candidates were able to write the compound proposition in words, however many were not able to write the converse in symbolic form. While they were able to fill in the third column of the truth table, many were unable to complete the fourth column correctly.
