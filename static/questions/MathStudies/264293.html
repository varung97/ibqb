Question

Consider the following propositions.

p : the baby cries
q : the baby is happy
r : the baby wants to play

Write down, in words, \(\left( {q \wedge r} \right) \Rightarrow \neg p\).

[3]
a.

Complete the following truth table.

[2]
b.

State whether \(\left( {q \wedge r} \right) \Rightarrow \neg p\) is a tautology, contradiction or neither.

[1]
c.
