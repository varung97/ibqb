Question

Consider the points A(1, 2, 3), B(1, 0, 5) and C(2, −1, 4).

Find \(\overrightarrow {{\text{AB}}}  \times \overrightarrow {{\text{AC}}} \).

[4]
a.

Hence find the area of the triangle ABC.

[2]
