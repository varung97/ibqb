Question

The three planes

     \(2x - 2y - z = 3\)

     \(4x + 5y - 2z = - 3\)

     \(3x + 4y - 3z = - 7\)

intersect at the point with coordinates (a, b, c).

Find the value of each of a, b and c.

[2]
a.

The equations of three planes are

     \(2x - 4y - 3z = 4\)

     \( - x + 3y + 5z = - 2\)

     \(3x - 5y - z = 6\).

Find a vector equation of the line of intersection of these three planes.

b.
