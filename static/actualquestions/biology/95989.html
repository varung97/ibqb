Examiners report

A2 (a) was well answered by most. Most candidates mentioned monounsaturated and polyunsaturated and some cis and trans.

a.

The majority of candidates explained the importance of fibre in the diet with in-depth explanation, although some candidates gave answers without biological jargon, such as "fibres clean the guts".

b.

Many candidates gave very long answers talking about the dangers of the fats associated to proteins (for example in beef) but did not make reference to the dangers of a protein-rich diet.

c.
