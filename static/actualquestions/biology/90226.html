Explain the importance of surface area to volume ratio as a limit to cell size.

Examiners report

Candidates scored marks for a good working, but did not measure correctly, so failed to obtain the second mark. This question seemed to cause some confusion with candidates measuring the picture rather than the scale bar. Also, many wrote down very large magnifications without thinking about plausibility.
