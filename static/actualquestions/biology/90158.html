Question

Which of the following events form the basis of immunity upon which the principle of vaccination is based?

 

 

Markscheme

D

Examiners report

Eight teachers objected to this question. Student performance on the question was poor with only 30% answering it correctly. The choice of incorrect response was spread between responses A, B and C. Students should have been easily able to rule out A and B as clonal selection is a clear part of the outcome. If students understand the clonal selection process correctly, then they would know that injection with an attenuated virus would result in polyclonal antibodies.
