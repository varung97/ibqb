Level Higher level Paper Paper 1 Time zone Time zone 2
Command term Question number 12 Adapted from N/A

Question

What do all human males inherit from their mother?

I. An X chromosome 
II. A Y chromosome 
III. Mitochondrial DNA

A. I only
B. I and II only
C. I and III only
D. I, II and III

Markscheme

C

Examiners report

Answers A and C were accepted as correct. As the origin of mitochondrial DNA is not in the syllabus, candidates could not be expected to know that mitochondrial DNA is only inherited from the mother.
