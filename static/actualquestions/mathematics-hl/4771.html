Question

The random variable X has the distribution \({\text{B}}(30,{\text{ }}p)\) . Given that \({\text{E}}(X) = 10\) , find

the value of p ;

[1]
a.

\({\text{P}}(X = 10)\) ;

[2]
b.

\({\text{P}}(X \geqslant 15)\) .

[2]
c.

Markscheme

\({\text{E}}(X) = np\)

\( \Rightarrow 10 = 30p\)

\( \Rightarrow p = \frac{1}{3}\)     A1

[1 mark]

a.

\(P(X = 10) = \left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}
  {30} \\
  {10}
\end{array}} \right){\left( {\frac{1}{3}} \right)^{10}}{\left( {\frac{2}{3}} \right)^{20}} = 0.153\)     (M1)A1

[2 marks]

b.

\({\text{P}}(X \geqslant 15) = 1 - {\text{P}}(X \leqslant 14)\)     (M1)

\( = 1 - 0.9565... = 0.0435\)     A1

[2 marks]

c.
