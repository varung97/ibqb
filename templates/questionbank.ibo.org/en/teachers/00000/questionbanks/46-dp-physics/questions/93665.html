Question

The peak value of an alternating sinusoidal potential difference is 100V. The approximate rms value of the potential difference will be

A. 50V.
B. 70V.
C. 140V.
D. 200V.

Markscheme

B

Examiners report

Far too many candidates thought that the rms value was half of the peak value.
