Question

Consider the system of equations

\[0.1x - 1.7y + 0.9z = - 4.4\]

\[ - 2.4x + 0.3y + 3.2z = 1.2\]

\[2.5x + 0.6y - 3.7z = 0.8.\]

Express the system of equations in matrix form.

[2]
a.

Find the solution to the system of equations.

[3]
b.

Markscheme

attempting to express the system in matrix form     M1

\(\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}
  {0.1}&{ - 1.7}&{0.9} \\
  { - 2.4}&{0.3}&{3.2} \\
  {2.5}&{0.6}&{ - 3.7}
\end{array}} \right)\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}
  x \\

  z
\end{array}} \right) = \left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}}
  { - 4.4} \\

  {0.8}
\end{array}} \right)\)     A1

Note: Award M1A1 for a correct augmented matrix.

 

[2 marks]

Examiners report

This was generally well done. In part (a), some candidates expressed the system of equations in the form \(XA = B\). In part (b), the overwhelming majority of candidates who used a direct GDC approach obtained the correct solution. Candidates who attempted matrix methods such as row reduction without a GDC were generally unsuccessful.
