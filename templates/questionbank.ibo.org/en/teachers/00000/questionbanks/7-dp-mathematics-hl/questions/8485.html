Question

The cumulative frequency graph below represents the weight in grams of 80 apples picked from a particular tree.


Estimate the

(i)     median weight of the apples;

(ii)     30th percentile of the weight of the apples.

[2]
a.

Estimate the number of apples which weigh more than 110 grams.

[2]
b.

Markscheme

(i)     median = 104 grams     A1

Note: Accept 105.

 

(ii)     30th percentile = 90 grams     A1

[2 marks]

a.

\(80 − 49\)     (M1)

\(= 31\)     A1

Note: Accept answers 30 to 32.

 

[2 marks]

b.

Examiners report
