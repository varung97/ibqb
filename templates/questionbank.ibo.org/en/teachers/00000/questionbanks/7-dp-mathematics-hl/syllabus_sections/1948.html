Topic 3 - Core: Circular functions and trigonometry

Path: 
Topic 3 - Core: Circular functions and trigonometry

Description

The aims of this topic are to explore the circular functions, to introduce some important trigonometric identities and to solve triangles using trigonometry. On examination papers, radian measure should be assumed unless otherwise indicated, for example, by \(x \mapsto \sin {x^0}\) .
