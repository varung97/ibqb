Question

The diagram shows the cross section of a plasma membrane.

What is found in area X?

A. Glycolipid
B. Glycoprotein
C. Polar amino acid
D. Non-polar amino acid

Markscheme

C

Examiners report

This was the third hardest on the paper and an excellent discriminator. Candidates were expected to identify the structure as a channel protein and deduce that polar amino acids would line the pore.
