El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO)

Path: 
Optional themes » Option B: Oceans and their coastal margins » Oceans and climate » El Niño Southern Oscillation (ENSO)

Description

Explain the atmosphere–oceanic interactions associated with ENSO.

Explain the El Niño and La Niña phenomena and their climatic, environmental and economic effects.

  • 11N.2.bp.3c: Discuss the effects of El Niño on some coastal margins.
  • 12N.2.bp.4c: Examine the economic effects of El Niño and/or La Niña events.
